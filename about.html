About

A home base built on care.

Hillview Residence is a small, managed residence in Cape Coast — close to the University of Cape Coast, and run with the steadiness of a place that is actually lived in.

Our approach

Quiet, secure, and well looked after.

We keep Hillview deliberately simple: a safe compound, clean shared spaces, and clear expectations that everyone agrees to before moving in. The goal is a place where students can focus on their studies and visitors can rest without friction.

The seven rooms carry Twi names — Aseda, Nhyira, Anidaso, Ahotɔ, Adom, Nkabom, and Gyedie — words for thanksgiving, blessing, hope, peace, grace, unity, and faith. They set the tone we want the residence to keep.
