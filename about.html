About Hillview Residence

A private student residence near the University of Cape Coast, designed for comfort, security, and a serious academic environment.

Our Residence

Hillview Residence provides a quiet, well-managed living environment for students and visiting scholars. The property combines privacy, convenience, and a peaceful hill setting with clear residence rules and structured management.

Our Approach

We prioritize safety, cleanliness, respectful community living, and transparent communication between residents and management.
