Frequently Asked Questions

Can students move in before paying their full balance?

No. Students cannot move in until the outstanding accommodation balance and the GHS 800 security deposit have been paid in full.

Does Hillview Residence assign roommates?

No. Applicants selecting shared occupancy are responsible for identifying their own roommate. If no roommate is secured by the move-in date, the applicant may be responsible for the full room cost or receive a refund subject to the refund policy.

What payment methods are accepted?

Cash, Mobile Money, and Bank Deposit. Students should submit payment details through the Reservations Portal.

Are two-bedroom apartments available?

Yes, two-bedroom apartments will be available for shorter-term stays. Online booking is coming soon; contact Madam Margaret for enquiries.
