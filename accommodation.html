What's included

Settled in from day one.

  • Furnished two-person room in a gated, monitored compound.
  • Resident Wi-Fi (for personal study and browsing).
  • Shared common areas kept clean and quiet.
  • On-site management and a clear residence agreement.
  • Overnight gate security; quiet hours from 10:00 p.m.

Good to know

A few house basics.

  • Residents cover their own electricity and refill their gas cylinder.
  • A refundable GHS 800 security deposit applies per room.
  • The deposit is returned where there is no damage beyond normal wear.
  • Move-in requires any outstanding balance and the deposit to be cleared.
  • The academic-year term runs September 2026 – July 2027.

Room rates depend on occupancy and term. For current pricing and availability, start a reservation or contact Madam Margaret on 024 221 5389 — quoted figures are confirmed by management before any payment.
